About Serapis Cyber

In 2022, Serapis Cyber was founded on the belief that traditional security models are outdated and do not factoring in the current threat landscape of quantum computing and Artificial Intelligence. The company's mission is to provide innovative, effective, and affordable cybersecurity solutions that protect businesses and consumers from the ever-evolving threat of cybercrime. Serapis Cyber's products are designed to stay ahead of the curve, with a focus on proactive rather than reactive security. This means that they not only protect against known threats, but also anticipate and defend against new and emerging threats such as Quantum, ML, and RF-based attacks.

In addition to our cutting-edge products & services, Serapis Cyber also offers a wide range of services, including threat intelligence, incident response, and forensics. When chosen for a given job we work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and objectives and tailor their services accordingly.
